## Restaurant Details

A hidden gem in Pasadena, Sichuan Street Food is that "hole in the wall" that you are looking for to recommend to friends; but only if you like spicy food.

*insert image here*

Come early as this place can have lines during peak dinner hours, especially on the weekends. I am a huge fan of their Spicy Chicken dish. I always order it in mild.
