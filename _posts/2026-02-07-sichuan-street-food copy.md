## Restaurant Details

Pasadena has many sushi restaurants and Osawa sits near the top, if not at the top, for the best fish quality for your dollar. Wheather its an omakase experiences, nagiri rolls, sashmi or sushi rolls, Osawa has some of the best selection in town for your dollar. 

Then also have a shabu shabu bar and a VIP room that you can reserve for larger parties and special events, although this may come at a cost.

Come early as this place can have lines during peak dinner hours, especially on the weekends. I am a huge fan of their Spicy Chicken dish. I always order it in mild.
