## Restaurant Details

Pasadena has many sushi restaurants and Osawa sits near the top, if not at the top, for the best fish quality for your dollar. Whether it's an omakase experience, nigiri rolls, sashimi or sushi rolls, Osawa has some of the best selection in town for your dollar.

They also have a shabu shabu bar and a VIP room that you can reserve for larger parties and special events, although this may come at a cost.

Come early as this place can have lines during peak dinner hours, especially on the weekends. Their sashimi platters and specialty rolls are exceptional.
